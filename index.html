About Me

Originally from Tel-Aviv, Israel, now living in Evanston, IL.

Curious by nature I love to search and discover unfamiliar subjects, illustrating and designing, and always occupy myself with projects.

I have a Bachelor degree in Landscape Architecture (2014) and 4 years of experience in Urban Planning as a Project manager. In addition I have an Associate Degree in Clinical massage therapy (2009). I served as a Military Commander and as a Company instructor for Special Forces at the Israel Defense Forces, Infantry (2005-2008).

Ohh, and apparently I really love birds.
